The program

Three phases.
One transformation.

The structure is the intervention. Each phase is harder in a different way — not harder to quit, but harder to stay comfortable with how you used to be.

Phase 1 · Days 1–30

Break.

The automatic loop ends here. Your chosen apps are blocked via iOS enforcement — no exceptions, no override, no workarounds. Most people feel the pull strongest on Days 3–7. That's the program working.

Phase 2 · Days 31–60

Build.

Every morning you answer one question: do I want access today? Research on habits and intentionality shows that deliberate choice can interrupt automatic behavior loops. You're restoring the agency compulsion removes.

Phase 3 · Days 61–90

Control.

Lighter guardrails, real proof. By Day 90 you have data: your craving curve, your journal, and a demonstrated ability to function without compulsive scrolling. That's not willpower — that's a new pattern.

How it works

Simple enough to start.
Strong enough to stick.

01

Pick your apps. Set a date.

No lengthy setup. Choose which apps to block, pick your start date, and add an accountability partner. If you go 48 hours without checking in, they'll know. That's the point.

02

iOS locks it. No workaround exists.

Enkrateia uses Apple's Family Controls framework — not a VPN or browser trick. The block is enforced at the OS level. You cannot override it, and neither can we. That's not a bug. That's the feature.

03

Check in. Watch your craving curve drop.

Thirty seconds a day: rate your craving, add a note if you want. Over time you'll see the curve fall. You'll have proof — not a feeling, but data — that the compulsion is loosening.

04

Read the letter you wrote yourself.

On Day 1 you'll write a letter to your Day-90 self. On Day 90 it gets delivered. That letter is the only review that matters — and you already know what you want it to say.

Privacy

Your recovery is
nobody's business.

We built this for people in a genuinely private struggle. Your data is treated with the same discipline we ask of you.

Your journal stays private

What you write is stored securely and accessible only to you. We back it up so you never lose your entries — but only you can read them. We can't. Nobody can.

App names stay private

Family Controls uses opaque tokens — iOS keeps app names private even from us. We never know which apps you blocked. Your specific struggle is yours alone.

Delete anytime

Delete your account directly from Settings — no email required, no waiting period. All data is cleared from our servers within 30 days.
